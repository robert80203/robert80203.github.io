Hello! My name is Shih-Po (Robert) Lee. I am a second-year PhD student at Khoury College of Computer Sciences of Northeastern University. My research directions are Video Understanding with Computer Vision and Deep Learning. I am currently working on error detection in procedural tasks, which aims to detect erroneous/abnormal action/steps in the process of performing actions to complete tasks.
