Hello! My name is Shih-Po (Robert) Lee. I am a PhD candidate at Khoury College of Computer Sciences of Northeastern University, advised by Ehsan Elhamifar. My research directions are Video Understanding, Computer Vision, and Deep Learning. I am currently working on ego/exo-view procedural video understanding and procedural error/mistake understanding using vision-language models (VLMs).
