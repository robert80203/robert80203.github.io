PhD Candidate · Northeastern University

Hello! My name is Shih-Po (Robert) Lee. I am a PhD candidate in the Khoury College of Computer Sciences at Northeastern University, advised by Ehsan Elhamifar. My research focuses on video understanding, computer vision, and deep learning. I am curretnly working on multimodal large language models and vision-language models for understanding long ego/exo-view procedural video.
